Nervix Release Readiness Checklist

1) Legal and store content
- Privacy Policy URL is valid and publicly reachable.
- Terms of Service text is up to date with app behavior.
- Medical disclaimer copy matches in-app wording and store listing.

2) Permissions and emergency wording
- Notification permission rationale is clear in onboarding/store listing.
- Emergency shortcut wording states it is not a replacement for emergency services.
- Region-specific emergency number behavior is verified.

3) Reliability and safety
- Crashlytics receiving non-fatal and fatal reports in release mode.
- Analytics events visible for reconnect attempts and emergency alerts.
- Reconnect/backoff flow verified under real network loss.

4) Data security and compliance
- Firestore rules deployed from `firestore.rules`.
- Health journal input length and allowed tags validated in UI and rules.
- Data retention/deletion process documented.

5) QA and regression
- Splash -> onboarding -> disclaimer -> login flow tested.
- Home abnormal alert -> history write -> emergency strip tested.
- Health notes create/edit/delete/tag + ordering tested.
